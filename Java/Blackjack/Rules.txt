Blackjack Rules
- Each player is dealt 2 cards.
- Cards 2-10 are worth their face value. J, Q, K = 10. Ace = 1 or 11.
- Player can choose to Hit (draw a card) or Stand (end their turn).
- Dealer must hit until reaching 17 or higher.
- Closest to 21 without going over wins.
- If a player goes over 21, they bust and lose.
