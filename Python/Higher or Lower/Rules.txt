1. The player is shown a card (numbered 1 to 13, where 1 = Ace, 11 = Jack, 12 = Queen, 13 = King).
2. The player guesses if the next card will be higher or lower.
3. A new card is drawn.
4. If the guess is correct, the score increases.
5. If the guess is wrong or the card is the same, the game ends.
